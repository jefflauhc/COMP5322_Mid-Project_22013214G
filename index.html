Self Introduction

Since my completion of Bachleor of Science in Radiography and graduated from The Hong Kong Polytechnic University in 2021, I have been working as a diagnostic radiographer in Tuen Mun Hospital, Hospital Authority. My routine duties include performing diagnostic imaging examinations in general radiography, fluoroscopy in Operating Theatres and Combined Endoscopic Centres, Computed Tomography (CT) and Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI). Also, as a PACS Administrator, I provide assistance to fellow colleagues in IT-related problems and ensure stable service of our PACS system for images archiving and transmission.

As a hobby, I have some basic knowledge in programming in Python, and web development in HTML, JavaScript and ReactJS. In my leisure time, I try to develop some web applications for my personal use, such as to read my weekly duty roster for work and then create events in my personal calendar to remind the scheduled duties. Through this subject of COMP5322 and this Taught Master Programme of Health Informatics, I aim to gain more knowledge in webpage development and other IT-related knowledge that could be applied in my job to assist the workflow of the department that I am currently working in.

Imaging Modalities

Tuen Mun Hospital provides various diagnostic imaging services and radiological interventional procedures:

  • General Radiography (Plain X-ray)
  • Fluoroscopy and Contrast Studies
  • Angiography and Interventional Radiology
  • Computed Tomography (CT)
  • Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI)
  • Ultrasound
  • Mammography and Breast Imaging
  • Nuclear Medicine and Positron Emission Imaging
