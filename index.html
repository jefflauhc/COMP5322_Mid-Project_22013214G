Self Introduction

Since my completion of Bachleor of Science in Radiography and graduated from The Hong Kong Polytechnic University in 2021, I have been working as a diagnostic radiographer in Tuen Mun Hospital, Hospital Authority. My routine duties include performing diagnostic imaging examinations in general radiography, fluoroscopy in Operating Theatres and Combined Endoscopic Centres, Computed Tomography (CT) and Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI). Also, as a PACS Administrator, I provide assistance to fellow colleagues in IT-related problems and ensure stable service of our PACS system for the archiving and transmission of medical images.

As a hobby, I have some basic knowledge in programming in Python, and web development in PHP and HTML. In my leisure time, I try to develop some web applications for my personal use, such as to read my weekly duty roster for work and then create events in my personal calendar to remind the scheduled duties. Through this subject of COMP5322 and the Taught Master Programme of Health Informatics provided by Faculty of Health and Social Sciences (FHSS), I aim to gain more knowledge in webpage development and other IT-related knowledge that could be applied in my job to assist the workflow of the department that I am currently working in.

Medical Imaging

Medical imaging is the use of technologies to produce diagnostic images of the human body to perform diagnosis by doctors, or to assist in treatments and interventional procedures.

The Department of Radiology & Nuclear Medicine in Tuen Mun Hospital provides various diagnostic imaging services and radiological interventional procedures:

Involves ionizing radiation Involves non-ionizing radiation
  • General Radiography (Plain X-ray)
  • Fluoroscopy and Contrast Studies
  • Angiography and Interventional Radiology
  • Computed Tomography (CT)
  • Mammography and Breast Imaging
  • Nuclear Medicine and Positron Emission Imaging
  • Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI)
  • Ultrasound
